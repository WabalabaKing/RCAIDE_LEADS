Notes

The Turboshaft class inherits from the Propulsor base class and implements methods for computing turboshaft engine performance. Unlike other gas turbine engines that produce thrust, a turboshaft engine’s primary output is shaft power, typically used to drive a helicopter rotor or other mechanical systems.

Definitions

‘ISA’

‘SFC’

Specific Fuel Consumption - fuel efficiency metric

‘OpenVSP’
